About AG STEM

Our mission: To show both girls and boys that girls belong in science and technology, too!

Founded in 2013 by Sean Reed, the Alliance for Girls in STEM is a publisher that brings to readers stories featuring young female protagonists who are passionate about pursuing careers in science and technology.

We believe that if we produce more books featuring young girls interested in STEM careers, we can build a large cohort of girls who will not have any problem self-identifying as someone who likes science and math, inspire the next generation of tech workers and leaders, and ultimately increase the number of women in the STEM-related careers where they are currently underrepresented.

Why Us?

We are dedicated to inspiring girls from an early age to pursue careers in the STEM field.

We believe the media we create will have the power to help more girls identify science as something that is not reserved for boys alone.
